Exploring Peru, One Adventure at a Time.

Travel agency, Peru Rafting Adventures, takes you where only the discerning dare to tread

From the heart of Peru, Rafting Adventures was born with the mission to share the beauty of our rivers and landscapes with the world. Our vision is to offer unforgettable, safe, and exciting rafting experiences. Rafting began in Peru in the 1980s with some of the first descents in the deepest Peruvian canyons.


At our rafting center, we take pride in being part of this passionate history. With expert guides, high-quality gear, and a commitment to safety and fun, we invite you to join us on an adventure that will take you down the same watery paths once traveled by bold pioneers. Come and join us as we explore wild waters, create unforgettable memories, and continue writing together the story of rafting.
