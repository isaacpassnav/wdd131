Project Site Plan

title

Peru-Rafting-Adventures

This name clearly represents the site's focus on rafting adventures in Peru. It is specific and exciting, which can attract water sports enthusiasts and those interested in outdoor activities

Site Purpose:

The site aims to be the primary online resource for rafting adventures in Peru. It will provide detailed information on the best rivers for rafting, available tour packages, professional guides, and reviews of other adventurers' experiences. Additionally, it will include a contact form for bookings and inquiries.

Scenarios:

  • What are the best places for rafting in Peru?
  • How can I book a rafting trip and what is included in the package?
  • What equipment do I need for a rafting trip and is it provided?
  • Is it safe for beginners and are professional guides available?
  • Are there recommendations for accommodation near the rafting areas?

Color Schema:

  • Navy Blue (#005B96). It will be used by Headings, Navigation Background, Secondary Buttons
  • Orange (#FF8C00). It will be used by Buttons, Links
  • Forest Green (#007F24). It will be used by Subheadings, Secondary Backgrounds
  • Light Gray (#E0E0E0). It will be used by Element Borders, Card Backgrounds
  • White (#FFFFFF). It will be used by Element Main Texts
